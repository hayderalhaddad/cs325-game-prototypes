  • Player Experience Goals: Players should find the game funny since it will be frustrating to win it. Players will also feel excited once they have actually won the game.
  • Gameplay: The player is a chick avaatar that they will move around to try and find and collect its baby chicks which are placed randomly in the map. The player's avaatar will follow the mouse pointer and accelerates towards it. While moving at very high speeds, the player will navigate through the tilemap to find its baby chicks. When a chick is found, the player's avaatar has to collide with the chick in order to collect it. Since the accelartion and max speed are very high, the player will be challenged to collide with the target quickly. Once all chicks are collected, the game ends, and the player is shown how much time it took him to finish the game.
  • Strategies: The player can try and decrease his speed by constatly accelarting in opposite directions which will help him gain control over his avaatar
